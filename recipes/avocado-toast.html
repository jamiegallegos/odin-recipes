Avocado Toast Recipe

Ah, avocado toast. A dish made with literally two relatively low-cost items you can buy at the store, yet a symbol for the luxurious and spoiled lifestyle of millenials of the world, meanwhile, a majority of the world's wealth continues to be hoarded and controlled by older generations. It's vegan, it's got fat, carbs, and some antioxidants! Plus, its obviously delicious, and for many, it's a classic staple of the depression snack repertroire. You already know how to make it, but this page exists for me to practice using HTML, so someday I, a millenial, can afford to have even MORE avocado toast.

Ingredients

  • Avocado
  • Bread (can be any kind of bread, bagel, english muffin, whatever you like!)
  • (Optional) Black pepper, your hot sauce of choice, sesame seeds, anything really! The avocado toast is a blank slate for your idea of a good time

Steps

  1. Toast bread to your liking
  2. Slice avocado down the middle (the long way, hot dog style)
  3. Remove pit
  4. Cut thin slices for easy spreading
  5. Scoop slices out of avocado rind and spread onto toast
  6. Mash or smooth to your liking on toast
  7. Add your optional spices or toppings
