Tofu Scramble

Despite being pretty easy to make, the tofu scramble is the kind of dish that makes you think "I'm a healthy adult" after eating it. Being a scramble, it's endlessly customizable. Add avocado, tomatoes, potatoes, mushrooms, sweet potatoes, whatever you have in the fridge! Truly, it's tasty, satisfying, and a healthy way to start your day.

Ingredients

  • Extra Firm Tofu
  • Spinach
  • Turmeric
  • Salt & pepper
  • Nutritional Yeast
  • Onion
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Saute diced onion until soft
  2. Add minced garlic to onion
  3. Squish tofu through fingers into pan
  4. Add spices. No specific measurements here, just do what feels right
  5. Add spinach, mix and let wilt.
  6. Turn off heat when tofu appears cooked (give or take 3 minutes) and add a generous amount of nutrional yeast
